A beloved board game - made digital

Quoridor is a fast-paced, abstract strategy board game where players race their pawns across the board while placing walls to block their opponents. With simple rules but deep tactical possibilities, the game challenges players to outthink and outmaneuver each other in a constantly shifting maze. Every move counts as you balance advancing toward victory with cleverly impeding your rivalâ€™s path.

The game starts out with the player on the bottom of the screen and the AI on top. The game duration and number of walls left are visible at all times.

You play in turns, moving or placing a wall.

At the end of the game the player can see various statistics about the match.
